How to use this app.

  1. Click the button of settings.
  2. Select County.
  3. Select Town.
  4. Click the button of backward.
  5. Click the button of "3 DAYS" then show every 3-hour weather forecast in the next three days.
  6. Click the button of "1 WEEK" then show every day weather forecast in the next seven days.
